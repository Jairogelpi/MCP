# Pricing Model

## 1. Self-Hosted (Open Core)
- **Cost**: Free (MIT License).
- **Features**: Basic Proxy, Rate Limiting, SQLite Ledger.
- **Support**: Community only.

## 2. Enterprise Standard
- **Cost**: $499/month per instance.
- **Features**: 
  - RBAC & Multi-Tenancy.
  - Audit Retention Policies.
  - Billing Engine (Settlement).
- **Support**: Email (24h SLA).

## 3. Enterprise Premier
- **Cost**: Custom (Volume based).
- **Features**: 
  - HA/DR (Multi-Replica).
  - Legal Hold & Forensics.
  - Dedicated Success Manager.
  - Custom Integrations.
