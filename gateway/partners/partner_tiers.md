# Partner Program Tiers

## 1. Registered Partner
- **Requirements**: None. Open sign-up.
- **Benefits**: Access to standard documentation, sandbox environment keys.
- **Logo Usage**: "Registered Partner" badge.

## 2. Certified Partner
- **Requirements**: 
  - Complete "Bronze" technical certification (conformance suite).
  - 1 verified case study.
- **Benefits**: Listed in Partner Directory, 10% revenue share on referrals.
- **Logo Usage**: "Certified Integration" badge.

## 3. Premier Partner
- **Requirements**:
  - "Gold" technical certification.
  - >$10k/mo influenced revenue.
  - Dedicated support staff.
- **Benefits**: Co-marketing, assigned solution engineer, 20% revenue share.
- **Logo Usage**: "Premier Partner" seal.
